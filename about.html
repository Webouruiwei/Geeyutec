About Loblich

A Global Brand of Kitchen Appliances

Design, innovation and technology. Loblich has designed innovative kitchen appliances for contemporary homes since 1998. Our manufacturing facilities combine traditional craftsmanship with state-of-the-art technology, producing gas cooktops that blend Italian design aesthetics with reliable performance.

Vision, intuition, international reach: owing to a business culture that places the utmost attention on quality, on technological contents with an extreme focus on energy efficiency, the Loblich brand is recognised internationally as a mastermind of excellence for modern kitchens.

Technology with Style

Loblich maintains strict quality control throughout the entire production process. Every gas cooktop undergoes rigorous testing to ensure absolute reliability, safety, and durability. What's more, thanks to increasingly cutting-edge engineering and different design solutions, every Loblich gas cooktop meets the various needs of the user in full respect of maximum simplicity of use and smart resource management.

Italian-Inspired Design

For Loblich, style is first and foremost a distinctive approach, creating gas cooktops that become statement pieces in your kitchen, combining functional performance with timeless aesthetics.

Quality Engineering

Certified manufacturing processes with in-depth material selection and precision engineering ensure every product meets the highest international quality and safety standards.

Sustainable Production

We are committed to environmental sustainability throughout our production processes, focusing on energy efficiency and responsible manufacturing practices.

Design

These days the ability to combine functional performance with superior energy efficiency is a must, a challenge that runs parallel with styling research that Loblich's advanced in-house design center carries forward while relying on partnerships with experienced designers worldwide.

Being able to team technology with style has made it possible to create products which have become genuine icons over time, appreciated by homeowners, architects, and kitchen designers around the world.

Italian Style Philosophy

The brand identifies with the features of Italian design: inventive elegance, originality, and quality products. This aspect is not just based on a question of styling but it is rather the expression of the origin of the Brand itself and of its passion.

Faithful to the philosophy of contemporary design, Loblich gas cooktops become objects capable of influencing the evolution of taste and of creating relations that go beyond mere practical requirements.

International Certifications

All Loblich products are certified to international safety and quality standards including CE, CSA, and other regional requirements for global distribution.

Global Distribution

Our gas cooktops are exported to over 50 countries across Europe, North America, Middle East, Asia Pacific, and Latin America.

OEM Capabilities

We partner with leading kitchen brands worldwide for OEM and ODM production, bringing your design vision to manufacturing reality.
