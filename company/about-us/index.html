The Loblich Group

Our Story

Founded in Foshan, the heart of China's manufacturing industry, Loblich has grown from a small workshop to become a recognized professional manufacturer of kitchen appliances. With over a decade of experience, we specialize in designing and producing high-quality gas cooktops that blend Italian aesthetic with Chinese manufacturing excellence.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great kitchen design combines timeless aesthetics with solid engineering. Every Loblich appliance is built to last, with attention to every detail from the first sketch to the final inspection.

Quality Manufacturing

Our modern factory covers over 10,000 square meters and is equipped with advanced production and testing equipment. We maintain strict quality control at every stage of production, ensuring each product meets international standards for safety and performance.

Global Reach

Today, Loblich products are exported to over 30 countries across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and Oceania. We work closely with importers, distributors, and kitchen designers to deliver quality appliances that meet local market requirements.
