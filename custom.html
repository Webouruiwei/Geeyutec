Custom Gas Cooktop Solutions

We specialize in custom manufacturing of gas cooktops for unique kitchen designs, OEM projects, and special architectural requirements. From custom dimensions to special configurations, we deliver quality tailored to your needs.

Your Vision, Our Manufacturing Expertise

At Loblich, we understand that every kitchen is unique. Whether you're an architect working on a bespoke kitchen design, a kitchen manufacturer needing OEM production, or a homeowner with special requirements, our custom manufacturing capabilities can deliver exactly what you need.

With decades of experience in gas appliance manufacturing and a commitment to Italian design aesthetic, we blend craftsmanship with modern production technology to create gas cooktops that perfectly match your specifications.

1

Consultation

Share your requirements: dimensions, burner count, special features, materials, and finish preferences. We'll review technical feasibility.

2

Design

Our engineering team creates detailed technical drawings and 3D models for your review and approval before production begins.

3

Manufacturing

Using premium materials and precision manufacturing, we produce your custom gas cooktop with the same quality standards as our standard products.

4

Delivery

Quality inspected, carefully packaged, and shipped to your location worldwide with full documentation and support.

Custom Dimensions

Need a non-standard size? We can manufacture gas cooktops in virtually any width from 30cm to 120cm to fit your exact cutout requirements.

Special Configurations

Any burner arrangement, combination with griddles, special cutouts for integrated ventilation, and multi-fuel options available.

Custom Finishes

From standard stainless steel and black glass to custom colors and special materials, we can match your kitchen design palette.

OEM & ODM Services

We work with kitchen brands and manufacturers worldwide for private label production with your branding and specifications.

Quality Assurance

Every custom cooktop undergoes the same rigorous testing and quality inspection as our standard products for safety and reliability.

Global Shipping

We export to over 50 countries with proper documentation, packing, and logistics support for international orders.

Ready to Start Your Custom Project?

Contact our sales engineering team to discuss your custom gas cooktop requirements. We'll work with you from concept to completion, ensuring you get exactly what you need for your project.
