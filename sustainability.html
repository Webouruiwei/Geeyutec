Sustainability

Our commitment to environmental responsibility and sustainable manufacturing practices for a better future.

Our Commitment to the Environment

At Loblich, we believe that excellence in manufacturing must go hand-in-hand with environmental responsibility. We are continuously working to reduce our environmental footprint through innovative technologies, efficient processes, and responsible resource management.

Our sustainability strategy is built on four core pillars: reducing carbon emissions, promoting circular economy, responsible sourcing, and energy efficiency in our products.

Carbon Reduction

We have invested in renewable energy sources for our manufacturing facilities, reducing our carbon emissions by 35% since 2020. We continue to implement energy-saving measures across all production processes.

Circular Economy

Our design approach incorporates recyclable materials, and we maintain comprehensive recycling programs for production waste. Over 85% of our manufacturing waste is recycled or repurposed.

Responsible Sourcing

We work closely with our suppliers to ensure ethical and environmentally responsible sourcing of raw materials, prioritizing partners who share our commitment to sustainability.

Energy Efficient Products

Every Loblich gas cooktop is engineered for optimal energy efficiency, delivering excellent performance while minimizing gas consumption without compromising cooking power.

Water Conservation

Advanced water recycling systems in our manufacturing facilities reduce water consumption by 40%, protecting local water resources.

Packaging Optimization

We have reduced packaging materials by 25% and use recycled cardboard wherever possible, with ongoing efforts to increase the use of recycled and recyclable packaging.

Continuous Improvement

Sustainability is not a destination but an ongoing journey. We regularly review and update our practices, invest in green technologies, and train our team in environmental awareness. We set ambitious annual targets and transparently report on our progress.

By choosing Loblich, you're not just getting a high-quality gas cooktop — you're supporting a company that takes its environmental responsibilities seriously and is working toward a more sustainable future for the appliance industry.
