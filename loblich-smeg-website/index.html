Our Story

Loblich is a professional manufacturer of gas cooktops based in Foshan, China. With over 10 years of exporting experience, we deliver high-quality certified cooking appliances to kitchens around the world. Every cooktop is engineered for performance, safety, and timeless design that complements any modern kitchen.

We believe that great design meets great function — delivering efficient heat distribution, easy cleaning, and lasting durability that stands the test of time.
