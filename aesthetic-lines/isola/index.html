Isola Collection

Born from the vision of Stefano Boeri Interiors

The Isola collection celebrates the island concept in modern kitchen design, creating a central space that brings people together around food and cooking. The design balances minimalism with warmth, creating a kitchen that's both contemporary and welcoming.

Design Concept

Working with Stefano Boeri Interiors, we've created a collection that respects architectural principles while delivering exceptional cooking performance. Each appliance integrates seamlessly into the overall kitchen design.
